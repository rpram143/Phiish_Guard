🎓 Educational Analysis: Red Flags in This Phishing Email

  1. Malicious URL: "Review Recent Activity" links to http://192.168.56.1:3003 instead of https://account.microsoft.com
  2. Generic Greeting: "Hello" instead of your actual name
  3. Fear Tactics: "account may be compromised", "personal data could be at risk"
  4. Suspicious Location: China - designed to create alarm
  5. Urgency Language: "Action Required", "immediately" to pressure quick action
  6. Unusual Time: 4:22 AM - odd timing to increase concern
  7. No HTTPS: Link uses HTTP instead of secure HTTPS
