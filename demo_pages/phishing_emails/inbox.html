🎓 How to Use This Demo

Scenario: You've received three "security alert" emails. They look legitimate, but they're actually sophisticated phishing attempts.

🧪 Test Without Protection:

  1. Disable the PhishGuard extension (if installed)
  2. Click on any email above to open it
  3. Click the action button in the email
  4. See how convincing the fake login page looks
  5. Notice how easy it would be to enter your credentials

🛡️ Test With Protection:

  1. Enable the PhishGuard AI extension
  2. Click on any email and then the action button
  3. Watch PhishGuard block the phishing page in real-time
  4. See the detailed threat analysis on the dashboard
  5. Learn why the page was flagged as dangerous

⚠️ Key Learning Points:

  • Phishing emails use urgency and fear to bypass critical thinking
  • Modern phishing looks nearly identical to legitimate emails
  • Always check URLs before clicking (hover over links)
  • Enable multi-factor authentication on all accounts
  • Use security tools like PhishGuard AI for protection
