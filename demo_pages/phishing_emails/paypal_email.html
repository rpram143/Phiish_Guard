🎓 Educational Analysis: Red Flags in This Phishing Email

  2. Suspicious URL: The link goes to http://192.168.56.1:3001 instead of https://www.paypal.com
  3. Generic Greeting: "Dear Valued Customer" instead of your actual name
  4. Threatening Language: "permanent account closure" to pressure immediate action
  5. Unsolicited Request: Real PayPal never asks you to verify via email links
  6. Poor Grammar (subtle): While mostly correct, some phrases feel slightly off
